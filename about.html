about unicon

our mission

Our initiative creates a platform for around 150 students from top tier universities, with backgrounds in software development, design and product management, to connect with industry leading companies and personalities - as well as with each other.

By facilitating this exchange, we want to support the next tech generation to foster positive impact on the digital community.
