about unicon

our mission

Our initiative creates a platform for around 150 students from top tier universities, with backgrounds in software development, design and product management, to connect with industry leading companies and personalities - as well as with each other.

By facilitating this exchange, we want to support the next tech generation to foster positive impact on the digital community.

the founding team

Unicon came to life more than two years ago, in October 2017, by seven people who lived, studied and went to many conferences together. At some point they realized, that they didn’t feel inspired by most of those conferences, because they didn’t meet many like-minded students and they also didn’t see all the topics they were interested in.
That’s when the idea of building their own conference sparked in their heads. They knew that it would give them a lot of creative freedom and they wanted to use it well. So they spent the following months working on a concept for the event they had in our minds, reaching out to potential speakers and supporters, finding a location, talking to catering companies and a lot more - they realized it was a very optimistic idea, to have only half a year to organize an event from scratch, but fortunately in the end, it all worked, and they had their first succesful event in April 2018.
